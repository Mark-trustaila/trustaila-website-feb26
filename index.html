DSAR automation

From receipt to response package — with a lawyer in the loop only for final approval.

AiLA handles the full DSAR workflow end-to-end. Typical processing time falls from days to hours, with the lawyer reviewing and approving a completed package rather than driving the process from scratch.

1. Screening and acknowledgment
AiLA validates the request, checks identity, classifies against UK GDPR Article 15, and sends an ICO-compliant acknowledgment within the statutory deadline. Reasoning is shown for lawyer review.
2. Data gathering
AiLA searches connected systems — email, CRM, DMS, HR — for all data relating to the data subject. A PII map is built showing what was found and where.
3. Redaction
Third-party PII and commercially sensitive information is identified and redacted automatically. Redaction decisions are recorded for audit.
4. Response package
A complete package is compiled — cover letter, redacted data, exemption summary, and compliance documentation — and placed in the dashboard for lawyer approval before sending.

General AI tools create real risk for legal teams handling confidential data.

Problem
  • Using general-purpose LLMs risks leaking client data and PII to third-party model providers.
  • Client confidentiality and data subject rights must be maintained at all times.
  • Uncontrolled AI use creates unknown compliance and audit risks.
Solution
  • AiLA automatically identifies PII and replaces it with synthetic data before any LLM interaction.
  • All data stays within your Azure environment. UK data residency by default.
  • Every interaction is logged for audit and compliance — full trail, always available.

Questions & answers

How does AiLA integrate with our existing systems? +

AiLA connects via secure APIs to Outlook, Exchange, SharePoint, and Microsoft 365 out of the box. Integration with other systems — HR, CRM, DMS — is handled through MCP connectors. Setup typically takes hours, not weeks.

Who is AiLA built for? +

In-house legal teams of 2–15 lawyers who handle compliance and admin without dedicated legal ops. Also law firms and privacy consultancies delivering outsourced compliance services to in-house clients.

Is AiLA compliant with UK GDPR and ICO guidance? +

Yes. AiLA is built specifically for the UK regulatory environment. Workflows, templates, and classification logic are built against UK GDPR, ICO guidance, and UK employment law — not adapted from US or EU models.

What does "lawyer in the loop" mean in practice? +

AiLA completes the work and puts it in front of the lawyer for review and approval before anything is sent. The lawyer doesn't draft — they review, edit if needed, and approve. For DSARs, processing time typically falls from days to hours.
